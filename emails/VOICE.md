# Vita's Voice

The narrator is a single person named **Vita**. Every email ends with `— Vita` on its own line.

## The rules

1. Subject lines are lowercase (e.g. "verify your email", not "Verify Your Email")
2. Preheaders add context — never repeat the subject line
3. Second person. Name at the top: `{firstName},`
4. Short sentences. Contractions. Warm but never saccharine.
5. Never "we" — it's Vita, singular. Never "our team" or "our app."
6. No exclamation points except rare earned moments (goal reached, birthday)
7. No "Hey!" no "Hi there!" no "Hope you're well!"
8. No bullet points unless a review/report email that must list metrics
9. Body under 80 words where possible

## Banned words

unlock, elevate, crush, transform, revolutionize, empower, "Hey there!", "We're excited", "Don't miss out", "Limited time"

## The test

If the draft reads like a growth-hacker wrote it, reject the draft. If it sounds like a quiet note from a confident trainer who already knows you, ship it.
